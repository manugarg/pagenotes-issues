Your Notes, Right Where You Need Them

Take notes on any webpage and see them instantly when you return. Simple, private, and synced across your devices.

Install Free Try Web App

Whether it’s a login ID, a membership number, a tracking code, or just a quick reminder — PageNotes lets you attach notes directly to any webpage. When you come back, your notes are already there waiting for you.

Features

Why Choose PageNotes?

Everything you need to stay organized while browsing the web.

Sync to Google Drive

Notes sync to your Google Drive automatically once you set it up. Never lose your notes and access them from any device.

Secure

Data stays with you — on your browser or Google Account. No 3rd party to trust. Encrypt notes with a passphrase for added security.

Organize with Ease

Find all your notes through an all notes page. Use tags or lightning fast search to find notes quickly.

Easy Backup and Restore

Take a backup of your notes and restore them when you need to.

Works on Mobile

Install the web app on your phone or tablet. Access your notes anywhere, no extension needed.

Multi Browser Support

Works on all Chrome based browsers (including Microsoft Edge, Opera, Brave, etc.) and Firefox.
