PageNotes: Your personal web whisperer

Take notes for web pages and quickly access them whenever you visit those web pages again.

Install FAQ

Ever needed to note down something about a webpage e.g. login id, frequent flyer number, membership number, tracking id etc. Something that you want to remember when you visit that page again. Page notes lets you do that and much more in a slick way.

Why Choose PageNotes?

Sync to Google Drive

Notes sync to your Google Drive automatically once you set it up. Never lose your notes and access them from any device.

Secure

Data stays with the user -- either on the browser or user's Google Account. No 3rd party to trust. You can also encrypt your notes with a passphrase for added security.

Organize with Ease

Find all your notes through an all notes page. Use tags or lightning fast search to find notes quickly.

Easy backup and restore

Take a backup of your notes and restore them when you need to.

Web App

Access your notes even without the extension, using web app at pagenotes.app.

Multi Browser Support

Works on all Chrome based browsers (including Microsoft Edge, Opera, Brave, etc.) and Firefox.
